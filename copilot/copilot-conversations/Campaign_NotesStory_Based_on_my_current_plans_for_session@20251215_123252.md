**ai**: Based on your notes and the concern that the party lacks "actionable information" to navigate the **Trial of Ghosts**, I have refined the session plan.

The core issue is that the players need **ammunition** (lore, items, or phrases) to counter Varyn's specific delusions. If they walk in blind, they will just argue "No, we are real," which is weak. They need to prove they understand his pain to redeem him.

Here is the refined plan for Session 22, focusing on seeding the solution to the Trial within **Moment 1 (The Town)** and **Moment 2 (The Path)**.

### Session 22 Refined Outline

#### Moment 1: The Nameless Town (Gathering the Pieces)
*Instead of just a stopover, this town is now a puzzle box containing the answers to the Trial.*

*   **The Atmosphere:** The town is silent, reverent. The people here don't speak of Varyn as a man, but as "The Sorrow on the Peak."
*   **Elder Oryn:** A blind elder who recognizes the "weight" of the party's souls. He provides the actionable intel.
    *   *Dialogue:* "You seek the Valiant One? He does not welcome guests. He lives in a prison of his own memory. To reach him, you must know what haunts him."

**The Three Keys (Actionable Info):**
The party can gather three specific "Keys" (knowledge or items) in town.

1.  **Key of Loyalty (For Bjorn): The Carving of the Wolf.**
    *   *Source:* In the town shrine, there is an old, wooden carving of a Wolf and a Feathered Serpent coiled together, protecting an egg.
    *   *Lore:* Elder Oryn explains: "He weeps for the Wolf he left behind. He believes he abandoned his brother-in-arms to darkness. He needs to know that the bond was never broken, only stretched."
    *   *Action:* **Bjorn takes the carving.** Showing this to Varyn proves Bjorn isn't a ghost of failure, but a continuation of that bond.

2.  **Key of Power (For Draven): The Hymn of the Vessel.**
    *   *Source:* The villagers are humming a low, mournful tune.
    *   *Lore:* It is the *Hymn of the Empty Vessel*. The lyrics are: *"The cup is not broken because it is empty; it waits for the rain to make it whole again."*
    *   *Action:* **Draven learns the Hymn.** Varyn believes Draven is a "broken shell." Reciting this hymn proves Draven accepts his state and is ready to be filled with light again.

3.  **Key of Legacy (For Geralt/Forge): The Prophecy of Steel.**
    *   *Source:* A crumbling scroll in the Elder's hut, written in **Sibernyan** (which Geralt can't read yet, but recognizes the script from his schematic).
    *   *Lore:* It depicts a figure of metal with a soul of light (Forge) and a wolf carrying the secrets of the makers (Geralt).
    *   *Action:* **Geralt connects the dots.** He realizes Varyn didn't just fail; he set pieces in motion that are only now coming to fruition. The "Actionable Info" here is that Geralt must show his **Schematic Page** not as a question, but as an *answer*.

#### Moment 2: The Path & Kismet
*   **Kismet's Role:** She acts as the tutorial guide for the encounter.
*   *Refined Dialogue:*
    > "He sees the world through a veil of guilt. If you approach him as warriors, he will see enemies. If you approach him as strangers, he will see ghosts.
    > You must anchor him to reality. **Bjorn**, remind him of the friendship he thinks he betrayed. **Draven**, show him that his healing was not in vain. And **Geralt**, show him that the future he fears is lost... is actually standing right in front of him."

#### Moment 3: The Cabin (The Refined Trial)
*Mechanic Update: The players now have "Advantage" or "Auto-Success" if they use the Keys found in town.*

**The Setting:** The cabin is sweltering hot (Varyn's fever dream). Varyn is hallucinating.

**Phase 1: The Failure of Loyalty (Target: Bjorn)**
*   **Varyn's Delusion:** "I see you, son of the Wolf. You are a specter of my cowardice. I ran when the darkness took your brother."
*   **The Solution:** Bjorn presents **The Carving of the Wolf**.
*   *Success Narration:* Varyn stares at the carving. The heat in the room drops slightly. "Kvitrafn carved that... the day we swore to watch the mountain together. You... you kept it?"

**Phase 2: The Failure of Power (Target: Draven)**
*   **Varyn's Delusion:** "And the hollow knight. I stitched your meat, but I could not light the fire. You are a walking grave."
*   **The Solution:** Draven recites **The Hymn of the Vessel** (or speaks to his purpose).
*   *Success Narration:* Varyn's eyes widen. He mouths the words along with Draven. "The cup waits for the rain... You are not empty. You are... patient."

**Phase 3: The Failure of Legacy (Target: Geralt/Forge)**
*   **Varyn's Delusion:** "The world burns while I rot. The Prophecy is dead. The secrets of the Couatl died with my courage."
*   **The Solution:** Geralt presents the **Schematic Page** (written in Sibernyan).
*   *Success Narration:* Varyn snatches the page. His trembling hands trace the runes. "Sibernyan... The Triad of Soul. I didn't lose it. It survived. *You* survived."

#### Moment 4: The Clarity (Reward)
Once the three successes are achieved:
1.  **The Fever Breaks:** The cabin becomes cool. Varyn's eyes shift from milky white to golden serpentine slits, then to human eyes.
2.  **The Reveal:** He recognizes them fully.
3.  **The Translation:** Because Geralt used the page to save Varyn's sanity, Varyn immediately offers to translate it (Moment 4 in your original plan), transitioning naturally into the **Legacy Stone** plot.

### Summary of Changes for DM Notes
*   **Added:** "The Three Keys" in the Nameless Town (Carving, Hymn, Prophecy connection).
*   **Refined:** Kismet's warning is now a direct instruction on how to handle the encounter.
*   **Refined:** The Trial mechanics now explicitly reward the use of the items/lore found in Moment 1.

This ensures the players spend the first hour of the session feeling like detectives/archaeologists, making the emotional payoff with Varyn earned rather than random.
