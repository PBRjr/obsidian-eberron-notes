# Agile Flyer
*Source: Flee, Mortals! p. 38*  
**Prerequisite**: Kite, harrier, or hawk
While in the air, the bird has a +5 bonus to Dexterity saving throws and Dexterity ([Acrobatics](Misc%20Files/CLI/rules/skills.md#Acrobatics)) checks.
