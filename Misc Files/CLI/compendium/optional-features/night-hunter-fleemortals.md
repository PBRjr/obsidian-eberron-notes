# Night Hunter
*Source: Flee, Mortals! p. 38*  
**Prerequisite**: Owl or letter-winged kite
The bird has darkvision out to a range of 120 feet and has a +5 bonus to Dexterity ([Stealth](Misc%20Files/CLI/rules/skills.md#Stealth)) checks.
