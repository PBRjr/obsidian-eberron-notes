# False Life
*1st-level, Necromancy*  


- **Casting time:** 1 Action
- **Range:** Self
- **Components:** V, S, M (a drop of alcohol)
- **Duration:** Instantaneous

You gain `2d4 + 4` [Temporary Hit Points](Misc%20Files/CLI/rules/variant-rules/temporary-hit-points-xphb.md).

**Using a Higher-Level Spell Slot.** You gain 5 additional [Temporary Hit Points](Misc%20Files/CLI/rules/variant-rules/temporary-hit-points-xphb.md) for each spell slot level above 1.
