**Saving Throws:** Str +11, Con +10, Wis +8  
**Damage Immunities:** Cold  
**Condition Immunities:** Charmed, Frightened  
**Senses:** Darkvision 120 ft., Passive Perception 18
 **Legendary Resistance (3/Day):** If Hrothgar fails a save, he can succeed instead.
 
**TRAITS**
- **Marshal the Corrupted:** Hrothgar and allies within 30 ft. have advantage on Saving Throws against spells and magical effects.
- **Hive Mind Sight:** Hrothgar perceives the world through the senses of any creature currently Charmed by him or bearing a Shard implant.

**ACTIONS**
- **Multiattack:** Hrothgar makes three attacks with [[Etherseraph]]. He can replace any of these attacks with **Shard Bolt.**
- **Etherseraph (Greatsword):** _Melee Weapon Attack:_ +11 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. _Hit:_ 13 (2d6 + 6) slashing damage plus 5 (2d4) radiant damage.
- **Shard Bolt:** _Ranged Spell Attack:_ +9 to hit, range 120 ft., one target. _Hit:_ 22 (4d10) force damage.

**BONUS ACTIONS**
- **Corrupting Gaze (Recharge 6):** Hrothgar fixes his gaze on a creature he can see within 60 feet. The target must succeed on a **DC 17 Wisdom saving throw** or be **Charmed** for 1 minute (save ends at start of turn). While Charmed, Hrothgar can mentally command them to use their **Reaction** to attack a target of his choice immediately The creature cannot knowingly harm Hrothgar on their turn while charmed.

**REACTIONS**
- **Shard Transposition:** When Hrothgar is hit by an attack, he can magically swap places with a willing or Charmed creature within 30 feet. The creature he swaps with takes the damage instead.

**Villain Actions (MCDM Style):**
1. **"Swarm Them!":** All **Shard-Thralls** move up to their speed and make one attack.
2. **"Consume Essence":** Hrothgar drains heat from the room. All creatures within 60 ft (Friend and Foe) must make a **DC 17 Con Save**. Fail: 18 (4d8) Necrotic damage. Success: Half. **Hrothgar heals 5 HP for every creature that fails.**
3. **"Detonate":** Hrothgar overloads up to 3 **Shard-Thralls**. They explode. Creatures within 10 ft of them make a **DC 17 Dex Save**, taking 28 (8d6) Cold damage on a fail, or half on success. The Thralls die.

## Shard-Thrall (Minion)
_Medium Construct/Humanoid, Lawful Evil_  
_Description: A Stormpeak refugee whose body is transformed by crystalline structures, which crackle with unstable purple energy. They twitch and vibrate violently._

**Armor Class** 14 (Natural Armor)  
**Hit Points** 1 (If they take damage, they die. If they succeed a save for half damage, they take no damage).  
**Speed** 30 ft., Ice Walk

**TRAITS**
- **Volatile:** When the Thrall dies, it releases a burst of icy energy. Attackers within 5 ft take 3 Cold damage.

**ACTIONS**
- **Shard Slam:** _Melee Weapon Attack:_ +6 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. _Hit:_ 6 Force damage (Static number, no rolling).
- **Shard-Group Attack.** If multiple Shard-Thralls attack the same target, they make a single attack roll. The attack bonus increases by **+1** for each thrall after the first. On a hit, the target takes **6 Force damage times the number of thralls**.
