## Blueprint Statblock
**Type:** Medium Humanoid (Corrupted), Lawful Evil  
**AC:** 18 (Plate) | **HP:** ~200 (Regenerates 10 HP if a Corrupted ally is alive)

**Traits:**
- **Ice Walk:** Ignores difficult terrain (Ice/Snow).
- **Hive Mind Sight:** Hrothgar perceives the world through the senses of any creature currently Charmed by him or bearing a Shard implant (Shard-Hulks/Hunters).
- **Marshal the Corrupted:** Allies within 30 ft. have advantage on Saving Throws (stolen from _Death Knight_).

**Actions:**
- **Multiattack:** Three attacks with his **Crystalline Greatsword** (deal Slashing + Cold damage).
- **Corrupting Gaze (Recharge 5-6):** Target one creature. Wisdom Save (DC 16-18) or be **Charmed** (stolen from _Aboleth/Vampire_). While charmed, Hrothgar can speak through them and uses their reaction to make an attack against their allies.
- **Shard Transposition:** Swap places with a willing/charmed creature within 60 ft (stolen from _Star Spawn Seer_).

**Villain Actions (MCDM Style):**
1. **"Hunt Them Down!":** All _Shard-Hunters_ and _Shard-Hulks_ move up to their speed toward an enemy. If they end within reach, they make one attack.
2. **"Your Strength is Mine":** Hrothgar drains the vitality from his minions. He heals 30 HP, and deals 10 necrotic/cold damage to all his allies within 60 ft.
3. **"Shatter the Weak":** Hrothgar overloads the corruption. One _Shard-Bound Elemental_ or _Hulk_ explodes. Creatures within 10 ft of it make a Dex save or take massive Cold/Force damage.
