#### The Shard-Spike (Weapon, Uncommon)

_Taken from the Shard-Hunters. It looks like a jagged ice-pick made of purple crystal, fused into a bone handle._

- **Weapon:** Dagger or War Pick (Light, Finesse).
    
- **Effect:** You have a **+1 bonus** to attack and damage rolls made with this magic weapon.
    
- **Passive:** When you hit a creature with this weapon, you can choose to leave a small splinter of crystal in the wound. The next time that creature takes **Cold damage** within 1 minute, the splinter shatters, dealing an additional **1d6 Piercing damage**. This effect can only happen once per round.
