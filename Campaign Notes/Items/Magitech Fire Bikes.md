### Mechanics

- **Stats:** AC 15, 50 HP. If reduced to 0 HP, it crashes. The rider must make a DC 15 Dexterity saving throw, taking 4d6 bludgeoning damage on a failure (half on success) and being thrown 10 feet.
- **Speed:** 120 ft.
- **Fuel:** Powered by a fist-sized Eberron dragonshard containing a fire elemental. One shard allows for 12 hours of travel per day.
- **Operation:** Requires an action and a DC 12 Intelligence (Arcana) check to start its engine.
- **Combat:**
    - While moving. 
	    - Melee attacks are made with advantage.
	    - Ranged attacks are made with disadvantage.
	- While at rest.
		- Melee attacks are made normally.
		- Ranged attacks are made normally.
    - Attack rolls against the moving motorcycle or its rider are made with disadvantage if the vehicle moved at least 30 ft. on its last turn.
