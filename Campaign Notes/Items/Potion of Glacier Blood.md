_A flask containing the processed blood of a Shard-Hulk. It is thick, blue, and sluggish._

- **Effect:** When you drink this potion, your skin hardens and turns translucent gray. For **1 hour**, you gain **Resistance to Cold damage** and a **+1 bonus to AC**.
    
- **The Side Effect:** While under the effect of this potion, your movement speed is reduced by **10 feet** due to your joints stiffening.
