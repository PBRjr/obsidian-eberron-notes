**Environment.**
* Blizzard -> [[lightly-obscured-xphb|Lightly Obscured]] past 10ft
* Ice & Snow -> [[difficult-terrain-xphb|Difficult Terrain]]
* Crystalline Monoliths
	- **Mechanic:** These provide **Total Cover**.
	- **Destructible:** They have AC 13, 20 HP. If destroyed, they explode (10ft radius, 2d6 Force damage).
- Howling Void
	- **The Save:** If shoved off, they make a **DC 15 Strength Save**.
	    - **Success:** They catch the ledge.
	    - **Fail:** They fall 30 ft down to a lower jutting rock, taking 3d6 bludgeoning damage and are removed from the main fight for a round while they climb back up.
- Risian winds
	- On even rounds, roll 1d4 at initiative count 20
		1. **Tailwind:** All ranged weapon attacks have double range.
		2. **Headwind:** All ranged weapon attacks have Disadvantage.
		3. **Whiteout:** Visibility drops to 15 ft. (Heavily Obscured).
		4. **Clear:** Wind clears for a moment
